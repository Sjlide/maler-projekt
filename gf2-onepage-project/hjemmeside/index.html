The story of Matluc

Mathi and Lucas were born on 10 August 2005, but only 16 years later the idea of ​​matluc was established, and motivation was set in motion with 3 t-shirts, for themselves and their father.

17 years old and filled with ideas, they have set out to complete their idea and dream of Matluc, but before that they need to have money to be ready to start the idea, Lucas and Mathi are in the process of educating themselves as web developers, in order to get the money for patent and production for the clothes and the ideas.

Getting the logo for the brand was a difficult thing for them, they had to have something that looked cool but also something that they themselves liked, they had many different logos to choose from.
